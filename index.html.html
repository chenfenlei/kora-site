Our mission

Senior care kitchens are running on empty.

The labor crisis in institutional food service is structural, not cyclical. It has worsened every year since 2015 and shows no signs of recovery.

How it works

Simple enough for any kitchen team.

01
~ 8 MIN
Load & confirm
Staff loads pre-measured ingredient stations. Kora self-checks, calibrates, and confirms ready status. No technical training required beyond a 2-hour onboarding.
Morning prep window
02
AUTONOMOUS
Kora executes
System follows the recipe sequence with sensor-confirmed task completion at every step. One staff member monitors, handles restocking, and manages exceptions.
Service period
03
AUTOMATED
Log & close
Compliance log auto-exports to kitchen management platform. Staff performs standard cleaning. Manager receives shift summary, yield data, and exception report.
End of shift

Built differently

Engineered for the regulated kitchen — not the lab.

Fixed-station design
No autonomy in unstructured spaces. The arm operates in a calibrated kitchen environment — making reliability a solved engineering problem, not a research one.
NSF + UL certified path
Built from the start to clear institutional certification. Food-contact materials are NSF-rated. UL listing in active progress with budget allocated.
Compliance, automated
Auto-generated CMS dietary logs every shift. Yield tracking, exception reporting, and audit trails — replacing hours of manual record-keeping.
Operational data flywheel
Every task generates sensor and outcome data. Models improve with every site deployed — competitors starting today cannot match this dataset for years.
OTA software
New recipes, operations, and integrations are delivered remotely. No technician visits. No hardware obsolescence. The platform improves while installed.
